Post-Matric Scholarship by the Government of India

Date : 11/01/2003 -

Funding Source: Central Government (60%) and State Government (40%)

Objective of the Scheme:

  • To enable students from backward classes to pursue college/higher education and to reduce the dropout rate among students.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • The student must belong to Scheduled Castes (SC) or Neo-Buddhist categories.
  • The annual income of the student’s parents should be ₹2.5 lakh or less.
  • The student should be pursuing education after the matriculation examination.
  • The student should be a resident of Maharashtra.

Nature of Benefits Provided:

  • Students are provided with maintenance allowance, tuition fees, examination fees, and other approved fees.
  • Maintenance allowance ranges from ₹250/- to ₹700/- per month (revised rates).
  • For students residing in hostels, the maintenance allowance ranges from ₹400/- to ₹1350/- per month (revised rates).
